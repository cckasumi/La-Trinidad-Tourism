WHERE IS LA TRINIDAD

La Trinidad is the capital town of Benguet province, Philippines.
It is located 256 kilometers north of Metro Manila. Its elevation is around 1,500m. The humble town promises a meaningful & memorable experience for visitors.
Come Visit La Trinidad!

ABOUT US

The Tourism Information Center is located in the premises of the Municipal Park. La Trinidad Benguet under the tourism campaign “valley of colors” hopes to sustain its role as a catalyst of progress, innovation, creativity and vibrancy.
