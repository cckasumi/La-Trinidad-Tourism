WHERE IS LA TRINIDAD

La Trinidad is the capital town of Benguet province, the Philippines.
The Baguio-Manila travel time takes about 7 hours by bus. Tourists can choose from three bus companies.

EVENTS

There are three major festials in La Trinidad every year.
The strawberry festival in March is especially famous. It aims to promote the strawberry industry and tourism of the valley of La Trinidad, Benguet.


  • 　　Coffee Festival

  • 　Strawberry Festival


  • 　　Adivay Festival
