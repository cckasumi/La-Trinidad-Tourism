VEGETABLE TRADING POST

This is a market that supports the vegetable industry of La Trinidad as Filipino salad bowl. There are a lot of colorful and fresh vegetables.

  • Vegetable Trading Post Movie

ABOUT LA TRINIDAD PUBLIC MARKET

La Trinidad vegetable trading post is located in pico. This is the first of its kind trading facility for highland vegetables in the Philippines.
Tourists visit the place primarily to buy fresh vegetables at a cheaper price and gain insight in to the operation of the facility.

ACCESS - FROM BAGUIO-

  • BY TAXI

    • La Trinidad Vegetable Trading Post-Baguio travel time takes about 15 minutes ride by taxi. The taxi fare is approximately 150 pesos.

  • BY JEEPNEY

    • The jeepney fare is 10 pesos. Tourists can ride a jeepney from station of BOKAWKAN(Burnham Park) and MAGSAYSAY.
